Delia Smith Fatless Sponge

Ingredients:

For the sponge

  • 3 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup plain flour (90g)
  • 1/2 cup castor sugar (100g)
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence (vanilla extract)

For the filling

  • 10 strawberries
  • 3/4 cup of cream
  • Jam of choice

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven: Heat your oven to 200°C (400°F). Grease and flour a 7” (175 mm) round cake tin.
  2. Sieve flour: Sift the plain flour and set it aside.
  3. Beat eggs and sugar: In a deep bowl, beat the eggs and castor sugar with an electric beater until thick and double in size.
  4. Add vanilla: Mix in the vanilla essence gently.
  5. Fold in flour: Carefully fold the sifted flour into the egg mixture using a spatula.
  6. Pour batter: Pour the batter into the prepared cake tin.
  7. Bake: Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Cool: Allow the cake to cool in the tin for 10-15 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.
